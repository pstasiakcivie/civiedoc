#  📂 Navigating to the Role List Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Role List">
    - Click on **Roles** from the left menu.
    - Select **Role Management** to access the Role List.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 🔎 Searching for a Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Roles">
    - Use the **Search Roles** bar to find a specific role by name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## ➕ Adding a New Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Add Role Screen">
    - Click the **Add Role** button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Role Details">
    - Enter the **Role Name**.
    - Configure the required **permissions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Role">
    - Click **Submit** to save the new role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## ✏️ Editing an Existing Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate and Edit Role">
    - Locate the role you wish to update.
    - Click the **Edit** button next to the role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Role Details">
    - Update the role’s **assigned users or permissions** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    - Click **Save** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 🗑️ Deleting a Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Role to Delete">
    - Locate the role you want to remove.
    - Click the **Delete** button next to the role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    - Confirm the action in the popup to permanently delete the role.
  </Step>
</Steps>
