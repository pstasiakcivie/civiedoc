# 🔐Permission Management

## 📄 Viewing Permissions

### Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Permission List Page">
    - Click on **Permission Management** from the left menu to view all existing permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a Permission">
    - Use the **Search Permissions** bar to find specific permission by **name** or **assigned users**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## ➕ Adding a New Permission

### Steps to Add a New Permission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Permission Popup">
    - Click the **Add Permission** button at the top-right corner.
    - The **Add Permission** popup will appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the Permission Details">
    - **Permission Name (mandatory):** Enter a unique name for the permission.
    - **Display Name:** Provide a user-friendly display name.
    - **Actions:** Select the required actions from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the New Permission">
    - Click **Submit** to save the permission.
    - Click **Close** if you do not wish to proceed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## ✏️ Editing an Existing Permission

### Steps to Edit a Permission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Permission to Edit">
    - Navigate to the **Permission List**.
    - Click the **Edit** icon next to the permission you want to modify.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Permission Details">
    - Modify **Permission Name**, **Display Name**, or **Actions** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    - Click **Submit** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## 🗑️ Deleting a Permission

### Steps to Delete a Permission

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Permission to Delete">
    - Navigate to the **Permission List**.
    - Click the **Delete** icon next to the permission you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    - A confirmation prompt will appear—click **Yes** to finalize the deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>
