## **Scope and Purpose**

Healthcare providers are struggling to keep up with the particulars of handling insurance and payments for their patients, increasing the demand for medical coding and billing solutions.

Rad Billing has been developed to meet this fast-growing need and maximize the efficiency of those processes.
