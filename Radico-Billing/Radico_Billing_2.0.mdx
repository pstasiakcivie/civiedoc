## **Scope and Purpose**

Healthcare providers are struggling to keep up with the particulars of handling insurance and payments for their patients, increasing the demand for medical coding and billing solutions.

Rad Billing has been developed to meet this fast-growing need and maximize the efficiency of those processes.

## **Process Overview**

Rad Billing handles everything from charges, insurance information, and claims, as well as bills and payments, including the follow-up required to receive payments or retrieve missing information. This is shown in the image below:

# Administration

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User" icon="circle-user" href="https://radicobilling.mintlify.app/Radico-Billing/Users">
    The **Users** section allows administrators to view, manage, and configure user accounts within the system. You can **search**, **filter**, and **export** user data, as well as **add new users** and manage existing ones. User permissions can be customized by assigning roles and access to specific **Organizations**, **Subscriptions**, and **Clients**. Additionally, accounts can be **disabled** when access is no longer required.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Permission" icon="shield-check" href="https://radicobilling.mintlify.app/Radico-Billing/Permissions">
