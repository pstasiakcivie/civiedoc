**Accessing the Ordering Doctor List**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Ordering Doctor Section">
    From the left-side menu, go to **Settings \> Ordering Doctor**.

    The **Ordering Doctor list** page opens, displaying all registered doctors.

    - Each entry includes:
      - NPI (National Provider Identifier)
      - Doctorâ€™s Name (First, Middle, Last)
      - Degree (MD, DO, etc.)
      - License Number
      - Status (Active/Inactive)

      <Info>
        **_Note_**_: Inactive doctors do not appear in claim entry dropdowns._
      </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Searching for a Doctor**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search by Keywords" icon="chevron-right">
    Use the **Search** bar to look up a doctor using NPI, name, or relevant keyword.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Applying Filters**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter the Doctor List" icon="chevron-right">
    Click the **Filter** button at the top-right.

    - Apply filters like: Status (Active/Inactive), Degree (MD, DO, etc.)
      - Click **Apply** to view results.

    <Info>
      **_Note_**_: Filtering is helpful in systems with large datasets._
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Updating Ordering Doctor Details**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh Doctor Profile" icon="chevron-right">
    - Select the doctor to update.
    - Click the **Refresh** icon beside their name.
    - The system pulls the latest data from the NPI Registry.

    <Info>
      Note: _Any updates from the official registry will be reflected._
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Enabling or Disabling an Ordering Doctor**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Change Doctor Status" icon="chevron-right">
    Locate the doctor in the list.

    - Use the **Status toggle**:
      - Purple = Active (available in claims)
      - Gray = Inactive (hidden from dropdowns)

      <Info>
        **Note**: _Disabling does not remove the doctor. You can reactivate them anytime._
      </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a New Ordering Doctor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter NPI Information">
    Click the **Add Ordering Doctor** button.

    Enter the doctor's **NPI** in the field provided.

    - The system may auto-validate against the NPI Registry.

      <Info>
        _NPI is mandatory for claim submission._
      </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Name and Degree">
    First Name (Mandatory), Middle Name (Optional), Last Name (Mandatory), and Degree (e.g., MD, DO, MBBS)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Licensing and Contact Info">
    License Number and Telephone (Format: (999) 999-9999)

    <Info>
      _Ensure the license is current and valid._
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Taxonomy Code and Status">
    Taxonomy Code: Defines the doctor's specialty

    Use the **Status Toggle** to mark as Active or Inactive
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Enumeration Date">
    Enter the **Enumeration Date** (MM/DD/YYYY)

    <Info>
      _Enumeration is when the NPI was officially assigned._
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or Cancel">
    Click **Save** to add the doctor to the system.

    Click **Cancel** to discard the entry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
