The Order Entry section allows users to upload, manage, and review order entry documents for patient processing. This includes adding new records, tracking document status, and managing related details.

---

## üìÇ Accessing Order Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Order Entry Tab">
    Navigate to the **Order Entry** tab from the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search">
    Use the **Search Bar** to find entries by file name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Click **Filter** to narrow results by criteria like status or date.

    <Note>
      - Use filters for quick access to relevant records.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Click **Export** to download the displayed records.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Order Entry">
    Click **View** in the **Actions** column to open order details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## ‚ûï Add Order Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Document">
    Click the **Add** button at the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Client Location">
    - Use the **Client Location** dropdown to select a site.
    - This field is **mandatory**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Document">
    - Click **Attach Files** and choose a `.pdf` document.
    - Max size: 5MB; Up to 5 files allowed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to complete the upload.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üëÅÔ∏è View and Manage Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Entry">
    - Use the **Actions** column menu and click **View** to open a document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document Status">
    - Status shown on the right:
      - **In Progress** ‚Äì Records are being added.
      - **Complete** ‚Äì All records are finalized.
      - **Invalid** ‚Äì Document is rejected.
    - To update:
      - Click **Action** \> Choose **Complete** or **Invalid**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Comments">
    - Click **Comments** to view or post feedback.
    - If no comments, the page shows **No comments found**.
    - Type in the field and click **Send** to submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## üìù Add New Record

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start New Record">
    - Click **Add New Record**.
    - Expand sections to enter required information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Patient Info">
    Fill out patient and guarantor details, including:

    - Name, DOB, Address, Phone, SSN, Medical Record ID, Employer
    - Guarantor Relationship, Contact Info, Address, etc.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Admission Info">
    - Patient Class, Location, Place of Service
    - Admit Date, Discharge Date (if applicable)
    - Referring Doctor Details
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Insurance Info">
    Provide primary insurance information:

    - Plan ID, Company Name, Group & Policy Numbers
    - Insured Details: Name, DOB, Address, SSN, Employer, Gender, Relationship
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  üí° **Note:** Expand each section to complete or review data. You may also add Secondary or Tertiary insurance if needed.
</Note>
