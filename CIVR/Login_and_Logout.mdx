title: "Login and Logout"
description: "Steps to log in and log out of the CIVR system."
---

## Login

To access CIVR, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your Username and Password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Log In">
    Click on the **Log In** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your credentials for CIVR are the same as those used for RadFlow.
</Note>

## Logout

To log out of the application:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open user menu">
    Click on your username in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Logout">
    Choose **Logout** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
