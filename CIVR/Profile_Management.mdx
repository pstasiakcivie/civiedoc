---
title: "Profile Management"
description: "Manage your CIVR user profile, update personal details, and reset your password or PIN."
---

## Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open user menu">
    Click on your username in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Profile">
    Select **Profile** to view your details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit user details">
    In the **User Details** section, you can update the following fields:\
    Username, Email, First Name, Last Name, Display Name, and Phone Number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing Your Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter current password">
    Enter your current password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set new password">
    Enter and confirm your new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply changes">
    Click **Reset** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing Your PIN

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter current PIN">
    Enter your current PIN.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set new PIN">
    Enter and confirm your new PIN.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply changes">
    Click **Reset** to update the PIN.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your CIVR password and PIN are synchronized with RadFlow and Radico. Any updates in one system will apply to the others, provided you are registered on both applications.
</Note>
