## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" />  **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** a patient, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist:

<CardGroup cols="3">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **MRN**, **Name**, **Date of Birth (DOB)**, **Phone Number**, or **Email**.
    - Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="filter">
    - Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Alerts, MRN, First, Last, or Middle Name, Date of Birth (DOB),** etc.
    - Click **Apply** to update the results based on the selected filters.
    - To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worklist Dropdown" icon="list">
    Refine the patient list using the **Worklist Range** dropdown and additional category filters.

    - Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (e.g., **Today**, **Last Week**, **Custom Date Range**).
    - Use the **Worklist Category** dropdown to further narrow down the selection based on specific worklist criteria.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Status Filter" icon="sparkles">
    Use the **Status Filter** panel to quickly view orders based on their current state‚Äîsuch as _Arrived_, _Checked In_, _Preparation_, _In Progress_, _Completed_, and more‚Äîfor efficient tracking and workflow management.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## üìù Editing Encounter Details

<Accordion title="Steps to Edit Encounter Details and Exam Information" icon="file-pen">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Access Encounter Details">
      Click the **Edit (pencil)** icon under the Encounter Details section of the patient's order.

      This opens the full encounter information panel where patient, physician, and exam data can be reviewed and updated.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Review Header Information">
      View key identifiers such as **Order Status**, **Accession Number**, and **Appointment Status**.

      Patient and referring physician information is displayed for quick reference.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Review and Update Reason and Symptoms">
      Confirm or enter the **Reason** and **Symptoms** for the order.

      Add any **Additional Notes** related to the patient‚Äôs medical condition.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Update Exam Details">
      - Under the **Exams** section, update the following fields:
        - **Exam Start/End Time**: Specify the time frame of the exam.
        - **Assigned Technologist**: Select the technologist responsible for performing the exam.
        - **Reason for Exam**: Provide a brief explanation of the purpose.
        - **Tech Comments**: Add any technologist notes (if applicable).
      - Expand additional fields using **Show More Fields** to enter:
        - **Diagnosis**, **Technologist Comments**, **Instructions for Radiologist**
        - **Prior Notes**, **Notes for Scheduler**, **Other Notes**
        - Select from dropdowns for **Admitting Physician**, **Admitting Staff**, **Admitting Doctor**, and **Admitting Technologist**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Manage Supplies (if applicable)">
      - If exam requires specific supplies, click **\+ Add New Supplies**.
      - Enter supply name and quantity used if available.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save or Cancel Changes">
      After reviewing or entering the required data, click **Save** to update the encounter record.

      - To exit without saving, click **Cancel**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Other Tabs Overview (Forms, Notes, Tasks, History)">
      **Forms Tab**: Displays patient intake or consent forms. Allows viewing or initiating new forms.

      **Notes Tab**: Contains any additional notes added by staff for context or instructions.

      **Tasks Tab**: Shows assigned tasks related to this order, including due dates and status.

      **History Tab**: Tracks chronological updates and actions taken on the order, including status changes, comments, and notifications.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
