**Key Features of the Patient Tab:**

> **Patient Worklist** – Displays a list of all patients with detailed information.

> **Search & Filters** – Helps users quickly locate patient records.

> **Editing Patient Records** – Enables modification of patient demographics, contact details, and insurance.

> **Adding New Patients** – Allows manual entry of new patient details.

> **Merging Patients** – Identifies and merges duplicate patient records.

> **Exporting Data** – Downloads patient data in .**xls** formats for record-keeping.



###

To **quickly locate** a patient, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="search">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    * 1\. Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **MRN**, **Name**, **Date of Birth (DOB)**, **Phone Number**, or **Email**.

    * 2\. Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="Filter">
    Apply specific filters using the filter panel on the right to refine the list further.

    * 1\. Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Alerts, MRN, First, Last, or Middle Nam, Date of Birth (DOB),** etc.

    * 2\. Click **Apply** to update the results based on the selected filters.

    * 3\. To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Worklist Dropdown" icon="list">
    Refine the patient list using the **Worklist Range** dropdown and additional category filters.

    * 1\. Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (e.g., **Today**, **Last Week**, **Custom Date Range**).

    * 2\. Use the **Worklist Category** dropdown to further narrow down the selection based on specific worklist criteria.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
