## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" />  **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** a patient, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist:

<CardGroup cols="3">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - 1. Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **MRN**, **Name**, **Date of Birth (DOB)**, **Phone Number**, or **Email**.
    - 1. Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="Filter">
    - 1. Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Alerts, MRN, First, Last, or Middle Nam, Date of Birth (DOB),** etc.
    - 1. Click **Apply** to update the results based on the selected filters.
    - 1. To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worklist Dropdown" icon="list">
    Refine the patient list using the **Worklist Range** dropdown and additional category filters.

    - 1. Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (e.g., **Today**, **Last Week**, **Custom Date Range**).
    - 1. Use the **Worklist Category** dropdown to further narrow down the selection based on specific worklist criteria.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## **Creating Customized Worklists**

The **Custom Worklist** enables users to efficiently manage, track, and prioritize patient details. By organizing patient details based on predefined criteria, the worklist streamlines workflows, and ensures quick access to patient information. This feature also supports automation, reducing manual effort while maintaining compliance and security.

<Accordion title="To add a new worklist, follow these steps:" icon="newspaper" iconType="duotone">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Access the Worklist Panel">
      Navigate to the **Worklists** page and open the panel from the right-side menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create a New Worklist">
      Click the <Icon icon="plus-large" />  icon to initiate a new worklist.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Worklist Details">
      Provide a **Worklist Name** and **Description** for identification.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select a Worklist Category ">
      Choose the appropriate category to classify the worklist.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Configure Access Preferences ">
      Enable the necessary checkboxes to determine accessibility:

      - **Add to Dashboard** – Display the worklist on the dashboard.
      - **Add to My Quick Search** – Include it in the quick search for easy retrieval.
      - **Add to Main Menu** – Make it available in the main navigation menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Define Filter Conditions">
      Configure **fields, operators, and values** to extract relevant data from the complete patient details.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Preview the Worklist">
      Review the filtered results to ensure accuracy.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save the Worklist">
      Click **Save** to finalize and store the worklist.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

## <Icon icon="address-card" iconType="duotone" color="#060606" size="27" /> **Adding a New Patient**

<Accordion title="Add New Patient" icon="newspaper" iconType="duotone">
  <Steps>
    <Step title='Click the "+ New Patient" button located at the top-right corner of the patients.'>
      - [While adding a new patient, click on **\+ New Patient**.](Add_New_Patient)

      <Frame>
        ![P1 Pn](/images/p1.png)
      </Frame>
      This will open the **Patient Demographic** page, where users can add new patient details:

      <Frame>
        ![1 Pn](/images/1.png)
      </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Patient Information">
      - Fill in the patient details in the respective fields. Fields marked with an asterisk (\*) are mandatory.

      <Frame>
        ![1 Pn](/images/1.png)
      </Frame>
      - Select the **Gender**, **Preferred Language, Race,** \*\*Marital Status \*\*and **Ethnicity**.
      - Check the **Is Blocked** checkbox to block a slot for the appointment. Then, add the reason for blocking the slot.
      - Next, select the appropriate **Patient Flag**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Address Details">
      - Navigate to **Address** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![1 Pn](/images/1.png)
      </Frame>
      - Enter the address details appropriately in the **Address 1** and **Address 2** fields.
      - Provide the **ZIP Code**, **City**, **State**, and **Country**.
      - Add the contact details in their respective fields: **Primary Phone**, **Secondary Phone**, **Email**, and **Alternate Email**.
      - Next, select the **Contact Preference** by checking the relevant options (**Text**, **Email**, **Fax**, and **Phone Call**).
      - Finally, choose the **Location Preference** from the dropdown menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Emergency Contact Information">
      - Navigate to **Emergency Contact** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2 Pn](/images/2.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Contact** button.
      - Provide a **Full Name**, **Phone** and **Relationship**.
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Guarantor Details">
      - Navigate to **Guarantor** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![3 Pn](/images/3.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Guarantor**.
      - Enter the guarantor’s details:
      - **First Name, Last Name, Date of Birth, SSN (if applicable)**
      - Select **Relationship with  Patient** and **Gender.**
      - Choose **Same Address as Patient** if the guarantor's address matches the patient's. If not, enter the address manually.
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Insurance Details">
      - Navigate to **Insurance** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![Insu Pn](/images/insu.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Insurance**. Fill in:
        - **Insurance Rank** (Primary, Secondary)
        - **Insurance Type** (Medical, Vision, Dental, etc.)
        - **Relationship to Policy Holder** (Self, Spouse, Child, etc.)
        - **Insurance Carrier** (Search or manually enter)
        - **Policy Number, Group Number**
      - Enter financial details:
        - **Remaining Deductible, Coinsurance, Copay, Out-of-Pocket**
      - Click **Save** to update the insurance record
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Employer Details">
      - Navigate to **Employer** tab.
      - Click **\+ Add Employer** button.
      - Fill in the following details.

      <Frame>
        ![Emp Pn](/images/emp.png)
      </Frame>
      - E**mployer Name**, **Website**, and **Phone Number.**
      - **Email**, **Alternative Email**, and **Fax.**
      - **Address (Street Name, Zip Code, City**, and **State).**
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Notes">
      - Navigate to **Notes** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 26 11 23 57 Pn](/images/2025-03-26_11-23-57.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click the **Add Note** button.
      - Type a note in the text box provided.
      - Select a note type:
        - **Important** (Red), **Warning** (Yellow), **Information** (Blue).
      - Click **Add** to save the note.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
