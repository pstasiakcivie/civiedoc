To **quickly locate** a patient, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<CardGroup cols="3">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="sparkles">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - 1. Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **MRN**, **Name**, **Date of Birth (DOB)**, **Phone Number**, or **Email**.
    - 1. Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="Filter">
    Apply specific filters using the filter panel on the right to refine the list further.

    - 1. Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Alerts, MRN, First, Last, or Middle Nam, Date of Birth (DOB),** etc.
    - 1. Click **Apply** to update the results based on the selected filters.
    - 1. To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worklist Dropdown" icon="list">
    Refine the patient list using the **Worklist Range** dropdown and additional category filters.

    - 1. Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (e.g., **Today**, **Last Week**, **Custom Date Range**).
    - 1. Use the **Worklist Category** dropdown to further narrow down the selection based on specific worklist criteria.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## **Creating Customized Worklists**

<Accordion title="To add a new worklist, follow these steps:" defaultOpen="false">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Access the Worklist Panel">
      Navigate to the **Worklists** page and open the panel from the right-side menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create a New Worklist">
      Click the <Icon icon="plus-large" />  icon to initiate a new worklist.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Worklist Details">
      Provide a **Worklist Name** and **Description** for identification.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select a Worklist Category ">
      Choose the appropriate category to classify the worklist.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Configure Access Preferences ">
      Enable the necessary checkboxes to determine accessibility:

      - **Add to Dashboard** – Display the worklist on the dashboard.
      - **Add to My Quick Search** – Include it in the quick search for easy retrieval.
      - **Add to Main Menu** – Make it available in the main navigation menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Define Filter Conditions">
      Configure **fields, operators, and values** to extract relevant data from the complete order list.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Preview the Worklist">
      Review the filtered results to ensure accuracy.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save the Worklist">
      Click **Save** to finalize and store the worklist.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

### **Viewing and Editing Patient Details**

<Accordion title="To view the patient details do the following:" defaultOpen="false">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Opening Patient Information">
      - Click on **View Details** to open the patient's information in a new tab.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Left Panel Options:">
      - **Edit Patient Demographics** – Modify personal details.
      - **Send Registration Link** – Share a registration link with the patient.
      - **View Balance and Patient Responsibility** – Check financial details.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Editing and Updating Patient Information">
      Users can update the following details:

      - **Active Insurance Details**
      - **Guarantor Information**
      - **Employer Details**
      - **Emergency Contact Information**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Adding Allergies">
      - Click <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" /> Add Allergies
      - Enter **Allergy Name**
      - Specify **Reaction & Reaction Code**
      - Select **Severity and Onset Date**
      - Click **Save**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Adding Medications">
      - Click <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" />  Add Medications
      - Enter **Medication Name**
      - Specify **Dose & Unit**
      - Click **Save**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Adding Medical Problems">
      - Click  <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" />  Add Problems
      - Enter **Diagnosis**
      - Select **Diagnosis Date** and **Problem Status** (Active, Controlled, Resolved)
      - Provide a **Problem Synopsis**
      - Click **Save**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Adding Past Medical History">
      - Click  <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" />  Past Medical History
      - Select **Start and End Date**
      - Enter **Description**
      - Click **Save**
    </Step>
    <Step title="Adding Past Surgical History">
      - Click <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" /> + Past Surgical History
      - Enter **Diagnosis**
      - Select **Start and End Date**
      - Enter **Description**
      - Click **Save**
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

### **Viewing Patient Details**

<Accordion title="To view the patient details do the following:" defaultOpen="false">
  Users can view patient details by clicking the **View** button, which opens a detailed patient information panel organized into multiple tabs:

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Overview Tab">
      Displays **today's appointment** along with a list of all previous appointments.

      - **Filtering Options:** Users can filter appointments using the **Search by Details** dropdown, **Date Range**, or a direct **Search by Details** field.
      - **Actions:**
        - **Add Order:** Click the **Add Order** button to create a new order. (_[Link to steps]_)
        - **Add Note:** Click the **Add Note** button to attach a note. (_[Link to steps]_)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Orders Tab">
      Allows users to search, view, and manage patient orders.

      - **Search Orders:** Use the search functionality to locate specific orders.
      - **Add New Order:** Click the **Add Order** button to create a new order. (_[Link to steps]_)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Insurance Tab">
      Enables users to search and view both active and inactive insurance details.

      - **Add New Insurance:** Click to add new insurance information. (_[Link to steps]_)
      - Click on the **three-dot menu** (⋮) next to the insurance entry.
      - Select **Edit** to update insurance details.
      - Make the necessary changes to the insurance information.
      - Click **Save** to apply the updates.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Click the three-dot menu (⋮) next to the insurance entry.">
      - Select **Disable** to deactivate the insurance record.
      - Confirm the action when prompted**.**
      - **Verify Eligibility:** Use the **Verify Eligibility** feature to check coverage in real-time and view the **Eligibility Response** immediately.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Billing Tab">
      Displays patient billing details, including existing invoices and charge summaries.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Making a Payment">
      Follow these steps to make a payment:

      - **Click the Make Payment Button:** Select the **Make Payment** button next to the invoice you wish to pay.
      - **Review Payment Breakdown:** A payment breakdown popup will appear, displaying the invoice details.
      - **Enter Payment Details:**
        - Input the **amount** to be paid.
        - Select the **payment mode** (e.g., **Card**, **Cash**, **Check**).
        - Enter the required payment details based on the selected mode.
      - **Submit Payment:** Click **Submit** to process the payment.
      - **Split Payment Option:** If needed, use the **Split Payment** feature to divide the payment across multiple methods.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Documents Tab">
      Enables users to search and view all documents linked to the selected patient, including scanned and faxed documents.

      - **Document Actions:**
        - **View**, **Download**, or **Delete** documents using the corresponding icons.
        - **Edit Document Type:** Click the **Edit** icon to change the document type by selecting the appropriate option from the available list.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Communication Tab">
      Displays the patient's communication history, including message chats and order follow-up messages.

      - **Send Message:** Click the **Send Message** button to initiate a new message.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
