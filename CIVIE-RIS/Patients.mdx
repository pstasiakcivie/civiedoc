# Overview

The Patient Management module in CIVIE RIS serves as the central hub for managing patient records. It provides a comprehensive worklist where users can search, filter, edit, merge, and organize patient data efficiently.

This module is used by administrators, front-desk staff, and healthcare providers to manage patient demographics, contact details, medical records, and appointment history.

## Key Features

* **Patient Worklist** – View and manage a list of all patients with detailed information
* **Search & Filters** – Quickly locate specific patient records 
* **Patient Registration** – Add new patients to the system
* **Record Management** – Edit and update patient information
* **Duplicate Resolution** – Identify and merge duplicate patient records
* **Data Export** – Download patient data in .xls format

## Searching & Filtering Patients

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use Search Fields">
    Utilize the search bar at the top to locate patients by:
    * MRN (Medical Record Number)
    * Name
    * Date of Birth
    * Phone Number
    * Email
    
    Results update automatically in real-time as you type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Filters">
    Use the Filters panel on the right to refine results by:
    * Alerts
    * MRN
    * First/Last/Middle Name
    * Date of Birth
    
    Click **Apply** to update results or **Reset Filters** to clear all filters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Worklist Range">
    Refine the patient list using:
    * Date range options (Today, Last Week, Custom)
    * Worklist Category selections
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding New Patients

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Registration">
    Click the **+ New Patient** button in the top-right corner to open the registration form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Patient Information">
    Fill in required fields (*) including:
    * Name and Demographics
    * Gender
    * Preferred Language
    * Race and Ethnicity
    * Marital Status
    * Patient Flags (if applicable)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Contact Details">
    Enter the following under the Address tab:
    * Street Address (Address 1 & 2)
    * ZIP Code
    * City, State, Country
    * Phone Numbers
    * Email Addresses
    * Contact Preferences
    * Location Preference
  </Step>

  <Step title="Emergency Contact">
    Under Emergency Contact tab:
    * Click **+ Add Contact**
    * Enter contact's full name
    * Provide phone number
    * Specify relationship
    * Click **Save**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Guarantor Information">
    Under Guarantor tab:
    * Click **+ Add Guarantor**
    * Enter guarantor details
    * Specify relationship
    * Add address (or use same as patient)
    * Click **Save**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Insurance Details">
    Under Insurance tab:
    * Click **+ Add Insurance**
    * Select insurance rank and type
    * Enter policy details
    * Add financial information
    * Click **Save**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Merging Duplicate Records

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Merge">
    Click the **Merge Patient** button at the top of the worklist.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Records">
    * Select checkboxes next to the duplicate patient records
    * Click **Finish Selection**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Information">
    In the Merge Patient popup:
    * Select which patient information to retain
    * Click **Next** to continue
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Complete">
    * Review the merged information
    * Add merge comments if needed
    * Click **Save** to complete the merge
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The merge process is permanent and cannot be undone. Double-check all information before completing the merge.
</Note>

## Viewing Patient Details

Access detailed patient information by clicking the **View** button, which opens a panel with multiple tabs:

### Overview Tab
* Today's appointments
* Previous appointment history
* Options to add orders and notes

### Orders Tab
* Search and view patient orders
* Create new orders
* Manage existing orders

### Insurance Tab
* Active and inactive insurance details
* Add new insurance
* Verify eligibility in real-time

### Billing Tab
* Invoice history
* Make payments
* View charge summaries

<Tip>
  Use the split payment feature when patients need to pay using multiple payment methods.
</Tip>

### Documents Tab
* View scanned and faxed documents
* Download or edit document types
* Manage document attachments

### Communication Tab
* Message history
* Order follow-up messages
* Send new messages

## Best Practices

<Note>
* Always verify patient information during registration
* Use the search function before adding new patients to avoid duplicates
* Regularly update patient contact information
* Document any changes made to patient records
* Verify insurance eligibility before appointments
</Note>
