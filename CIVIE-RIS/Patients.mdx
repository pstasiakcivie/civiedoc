# Patient Management Overview

The Patient Management module in CIVIE RIS provides a centralized system for managing patient records, demographics, medical history, and related information. This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of patient management functionality.

## Patient Worklist

The Patient Worklist serves as your main dashboard for accessing and managing patient records.

## Managing Patient Records

### Adding a New Patient

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access New Patient Form">
    Click the "+ New Patient" button in the top-right corner of the Patient Worklist
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Demographics">
    Fill in required fields:
    - First and Last Name
    - Date of Birth
    - Gender
    - Preferred Language
    - Race and Ethnicity
    - Marital Status
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact Information">
    Provide:
    - Primary Address
    - Phone Numbers
    - Email Addresses
    - Contact Preferences
  </Step>

  <Step title="Additional Details">
    Add optional information:
    - Emergency Contacts
    - Insurance Details
    - Employer Information
    - Patient Notes
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing Patient Information

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Demographics">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Edit Mode">
        Click the Edit icon next to the patient's record
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update Fields">
        Modify any of the following:
        - Personal Information
        - Contact Details
        - Language Preferences
        - Demographics
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save Changes">
        Click "Save" to apply updates
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Insurance">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add New Insurance">
        Click "+ Add Insurance" in the Insurance tab
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Details">
        Provide:
        - Insurance Carrier
        - Policy Information
        - Coverage Details
        - Verification Status
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Coverage">
        Use "Verify Eligibility" to check insurance status
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Advanced Features

### Merging Patient Records

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Merge">
    Click "Merge Patient" in the top menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Records">
    Choose two patient records to merge
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Information">
    Compare and select which information to retain
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Merge">
    Add any necessary notes and confirm the merge
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Merging patient records cannot be undone. Please review all information carefully before confirming.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  ### Recommended Workflows

  - Verify patient information at each visit
  - Regularly update insurance information
  - Document all communication attempts
  - Maintain detailed notes for special circumstances
</Tip>

<Note>
  ### Data Security

  All patient information is protected under HIPAA guidelines. Ensure you:
  - Log out after each session
  - Never share login credentials
  - Report any suspicious activity
  - Follow all security protocols
</Note>
