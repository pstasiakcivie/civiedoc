The **Incoming Documents** page consists of the following sections:

> **Summary- Displays the** **total count of documents received  based on different time frames:**
>
> - **All Documents** – Total number of received documents.
> - **Today** – Documents received on the current day.
> - **Yesterday** – Documents received on the previous day.
> - **This Week** – Documents received during the current week.
> - **This Month** – Documents received during the current month.

> **Shared Worklis**-My Personal Worklist- Displays documents that are assigned specifically to the logged-in user. These documents are designated for individual processing and responsibility. Users can view, verify, and take action on the assigned documents, ensuring accountability and efficient management of their workload.

> **Shared Worklis**-Contains documents that are accessible to multiple users for collaborative review and processing. This worklist allows different team members to work on the same set of documents, ensuring that urgent or high-priority items can be addressed efficiently by any available user. It enhances teamwork and flexibility by enabling document handling without dependency on a single user.

    - 1. Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="Filter">

    - 1. Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Source, Document, Location, File name etc.**
      2. ![images/incomingdocumentfilter1.png](/images/incomingdocumentfilter1.png)
