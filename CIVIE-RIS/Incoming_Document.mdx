## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" />  **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** a document, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Fields">
    \- Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a document by entering **Document Name, Location, or File Name**.
    \- Enter the search query, and results will update in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Panel">
    \- Click on the **Filters** panel and select from the available options: **Alerts, MRN, Name, DOB, etc.**.
    \- Click **Apply** to refine the results or **Reset Filters** to clear them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Worklist Dropdown">
    \- Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (**Today, Last Week, Custom Date Range**).
    \- Use **Worklist Category** to further narrow down the results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## <Icon icon="list-ul" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" /> **Creating Custom Worklists**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Worklist Panel">
    Navigate to the **Worklists** page and open the panel from the right-side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Worklist">
    Click the **+** icon to initiate a new worklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Worklist Details">
    Provide a **Worklist Name** and **Description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Access Preferences">
    \- Enable **Add to Dashboard, Quick Search, or Main Menu** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Filter Conditions">
    Configure **fields, operators, and values** to refine data selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Worklist">
    Click **SAVE** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## <Icon icon="pencil" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="26" />  **Verify & Edit Document Details**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Edit (✏️) icon">
    <CardGroup cols="2">
      <Card title="Left Panel " icon="left">
        **Document Preview:** Displays the scanned/faxed document.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Right Panel" icon="Right">
        **Document Details:** Shows metadata such as:

        📑 **Document Number & Date Received** 👤 **Created By & Modified Date**

        📄 **Document Type** (e.g., RX, Face Sheet)
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Document Type">
    \- Select **Rx Medical Order, Facesheet, Insurance Card**, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Patient Information">
    \- Manually enter details retrieved from the document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order Detail Field">
    \- Select, search, or add a new order.
    \- Use <Icon icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="solid" /> to search by **Exam Code, Procedure, CPT Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Insurance Information">
    \- Select existing insurance or add a new entry.\
    \- Click **Verify Eligibility** to check status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Managing Alerts & Tags">
    \- **Alert Flags**: Displays patient conditions (e.g., **Wheelchair, Smoker**).
    \- **Tags**: Categorizes exams or conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Button">
    \- Click **Complete** to finalize or select another status (**Escalate, Tech Review, Client Review**).
  </Step>
</Steps>
