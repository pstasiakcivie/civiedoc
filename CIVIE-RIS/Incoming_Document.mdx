This module helps streamline document workflows and ensures that important medical records are efficiently handled. Based on these documents, relevant data is entered into the system database."

---

The **Incoming Documents** page consists of the following sections:

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Summary" icon="filter">
    **Displays the total count of received documents based on different time frames:**

    - **All Documents** – Total number of received documents.
    - **Today** – Documents received on the current day.
    - **Yesterday** – Documents received on the previous day.
    - **This Week** – Documents received during the current week.
    - **This Month** – Documents received during the current month.
  </Card>
  <Card title="My Personal Worklist" icon="LIST">
    Displays documents that are assigned specifically to the logged-in user. These documents are designated for individual processing and responsibility. Users can view, verify, and take action on the assigned documents, ensuring accountability and efficient management of their workload.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shared Worklist" icon="share">
    Contains documents that are accessible to multiple users for collaborative review and processing. This worklist allows different team members to work on the same set of documents, ensuring that urgent or high-priority items can be addressed efficiently by any available user. It enhances teamwork and flexibility by enabling document handling without dependency on a single user.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" />  **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** a document, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Fields">
    - Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a document by entering **Document Name, Location, or File Name**.
    - Enter the search query, and results will update in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Panel">
    - Click on the **Filters** panel and select from the available options: **Alerts, MRN, Name, DOB, etc.**
    - Click **Apply** to refine the results or **Reset Filters** to clear them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Worklist Dropdown">
    - Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (**Today, Last Week, Custom Date Range**).
    - Use **Worklist Category** to further narrow down the results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## <Icon icon="list-ul" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" /> **Creating Custom Worklists**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Worklist Panel">
    Navigate to the **Worklists** page and open the panel from the right-side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Worklist">
    Click the **+** icon to initiate a new worklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Worklist Details">
    Provide a **Worklist Name** and **Description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Access Preferences">
    Enable **Add to Dashboard, Quick Search, or Main Menu** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Filter Conditions">
    Configure **fields, operators, and values** to refine data selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Worklist">
    Click **SAVE** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>
