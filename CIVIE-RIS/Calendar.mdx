# Calendar Overview

The **Calendar** module in CIVIE RIS provides a centralized scheduling system for managing patient appointments, resource availability, and location-specific scheduling. It enables users to efficiently view, create, edit, and manage appointment slots for radiology and medical practices.

## Key Features

- **Appointment Scheduling** – Create, modify, reschedule, and cancel appointments
- **Resource & Location Management** – Assign appointments to specific facilities, rooms, and modalities
- **Filters & Search** – Quick filtering by location, resource, status, and captions
- **View Options** – Switch between day, week, list, and status views
- **Blocked Slots Management** – Track unavailable slots due to holidays or tech unavailability
- **Waitlist Management** – Manage patient waitlists for available slots

## Filtering Appointments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filters Panel">
    Click the Filters panel on the left side of the calendar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Location and Resource">
    Choose the required location and resource from the dropdown menus
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Appointment Status">
    Filter by status (e.g., Scheduled, Cancelled)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Modify Captions">
    Customize the display of patient information on appointment cards
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Filtered Results">
    Calendar appointments are automatically filtered based on your selections
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reset Filters">
    Click "Reset" to clear all applied filters
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a New Appointment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate New Appointment">
    Click the "+ New Appointment" button in the top-right corner to open the Patient Details page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Order Type">
    Choose from available options:
    - Patient Self-Schedule
    - Follow-Up Order
    - Phone Order
    - Walk-In Order
    - Physician Portal
    - EMR Orders
    - BCS Measure
  </Step>

  <Step title="Patient Information">
    - Search for existing patient or click "+ New Patient"
    - Fill in required fields (marked with *)
    - Select Gender, Preferred Language, Race, Marital Status, and Ethnicity
    - Set Patient Flags if applicable
  </Step>

  <Step title="Address Details">
    - Enter primary and secondary address
    - Provide contact information (phone, email)
    - Set contact preferences (Text, Email, Fax, Phone Call)
    - Choose Location Preference
  </Step>

  <Step title="Emergency Contact">
    - Click "+ Add Contact"
    - Enter Full Name, Phone, and Relationship
    - Save the contact information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Guarantor Details">
    - Add guarantor information
    - Specify relationship to patient
    - Enter contact details
    - Set address (same as patient or different)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Insurance Information">
    - Add primary and secondary insurance
    - Enter policy details
    - Specify financial information
    - Save insurance record
  </Step>

  <Step title="Employer Information">
    - Add employer details
    - Enter contact information
    - Provide address details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Additional Notes">
    - Add relevant notes
    - Select note type (Important, Warning, Information)
    - Save notes and confirm appointment
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Right-Click Actions

The Calendar provides context-specific actions through right-click menus on appointments and time slots.

### Available Slot Actions

When right-clicking on an available slot, you can:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Appointment Booking" icon="calendar-plus">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Booking">
        Right-click the "+" icon and select "Appointment Booking"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Patient">
        Choose existing patient or add new
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Referring Physician">
        Search or add new referring physician
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Exams">
        Add required examination details
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review Documents">
        Check available patient documents
      </Step>
      <Step title="Submit">
        Confirm and create the appointment
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Block Slot" icon="ban">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Initialize">
        Right-click and select "Block Slot"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Details">
        - Add title and description
        - Select occurrence type (Weekly/Monthly/Custom)
        - Set date and time range
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        Click "Block" to finalize
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Open Slot" icon="door-open">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access">
        Right-click and choose "Open Slot"
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure">
        - Enter title and description
        - Select location
        - Choose occurrence pattern
        - Set time range
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Click "Submit" to confirm
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Managing Existing Appointments

When right-clicking on a scheduled appointment, you can update its status to:

- Confirm
- Checked In
- Preparation
- Tech Acknowledged
- In Progress
- Complete
- No Show
- Cancelled
- Arrived
- On Hold
- Running Late

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Appointment">
    Click on the appointment to update
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Status">
    Right-click and select the appropriate status
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Notes">
    Enter any necessary notes in the confirmation dialog
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click "Yes" to update the status
  </Step>
</Steps>
