## Filtering Appointments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Filters Panel">
    Navigate to the left side of the calendar and click on the **Filters Panel** to expand it.

    <img
      src="/images/civie.png"
      alt="Civie Pn"
      title="Civie Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"89%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Required Filters">
    Choose the necessary **Location** and **Resource** to refine your search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Appointment Status">
    Select from available statuses like **Scheduled, Cancelled, or Pending** to filter relevant appointments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Captions">
    Customize captions to display specific details on **patient cards**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply or Reset Filters">
    Once selected, the calendar will automatically update to reflect the applied filters.

    Click **Reset** to clear all filters and return to the default view.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  ## **Best Practices**

  Utilizing filters can help you quickly locate patient appointments and improve scheduling efficiency.
</Tip>

Users can **right-click** on any appointment or blocked slot to access various actions, such as **declining, deleting, or updating appointment statuses**. Additionally, right-clicking on a **blank slot** allows users to manage scheduling with options like **booking appointments, blocking slots, and accessing waitlists**.

## **Sending Check-In Link & QR Code**

The **CIVIE Calendar** allows users to send a **Check-In Link** or generate a **QR code** for patients to check in seamlessly.

**Quick Access:** Patients can check in using a direct link sent via **Text or Email**, or by scanning a **QR code**.

<Accordion title="Sending Check-In Link to Patient" defaultOpen="false">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to Appointment Summary">
      Open the **Appointment Summary** panel. Locate the **Booking Details** section.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Send Check-In Link">
      Click on **Send Check-In Link**.

      <img
        src="/images/image(10).jpg"
        alt=""
        className="mx-auto"
        style={{ width:"79%" }}
      />

      A pop-up titled **Send form Link to Patient** will appear.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Choose Delivery Method">
      **Text Message:** Enter the patient’s phone number.

      **Email:** Enter the patient’s email address.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Confirm and Send">
      Click **Send** to share the link with the patient. The patient receives the link and can check in directly.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
  <Tip>
    ## **Best Practices**

    ✅ Reduces waiting time at the front desk.

    ✅Benefits of sending Checks-In-links

    ✅ Ensures a seamless check-in process.

    ✅ Allows patients to update information remotely.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Generating & Viewing Check-In QR Code" defaultOpen="false">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to Appointment Summary">
      Open the **Appointment Summary** panel. Locate the **Booking Details** section.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Open QR Code">
      Click on the **QR Code Icon** next to "Send Check-In Link".

      <img
        src="/images/image(13).jpg"
        alt=""
        style={{ width:"85%" }}
      />

      A pop-up titled **Check-In QR Code** will appear.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Patient Scan & Check-In">
      The patient can scan the **QR Code** using their mobile device. Alternatively, they can click on **Click here to go for check-in** below the QR code.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
  <Tip>
    ## **Best Practices**

    ✅ Ensure the patient receives either the **check-in link** or **QR code** well before their appointment to avoid delays.

    ✅ These steps ensure a seamless patient check-in process via **direct links or QR scanning**.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>
