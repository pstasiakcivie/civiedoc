> **Summary- Displays the** **total count of orders received  based on different time frames:**
>
> - **All Documents** – Total number of received orders.
> - **Today** – Orders received on the current day.
> - **Tommorrow** – Orders to be received on the next day.
> - **This Week** – Orders received during the current week.

> Beneath the summary, it shows all the personalized worklists created by the logged-in user. These worklists are easy to access and manage, allowing users to view, verify, and take necessary actions effortlessly. This ensures better control, accountability, and efficient handling of orders.

<Accordion title="Accessing the Orders Worklist" icon="List">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to Orders Worklist ">
      Click on **Orders Worklist** tab from the left-side menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Apply Filters ">
      Refine the order list on your \*\*dashboard \*\*by choosing one of the following options:

      - **All Orders**
      - **Today**
      - **This Week**

      You can also select a **custom worklist** shown below the summary to view its associated orders.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

## <Icon icon="address-card" iconType="duotone" color="#090909" size="27" /> **Adding a New Order**

<Accordion title="To add a new order" icon="file-arrow-down" iconType="duotone">
  <Steps>
    <Step title='Click the "+ New Order" button located at the top-right corner of the Orders Worklist'>
      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 25 09 34 09 Pn](/images/2025-03-25_09-34-09.png)
      </Frame>
      This will open the **Overview** page, where users can:

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 09 33 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-09-33.png)
      </Frame>
      - **Search for an existing patient** or **add a new patient** to create an appointment.
      - **Select the order type** for which the appointment needs to be scheduled.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Access the Patient Details Form">
      - Click on the \*\*Order Type \*\*dropdown and select the appropriate order, by default selected order type is online order. User can change the order to – **Patient Self-Schedule** , **Follow-Up Order** , **Phone Order**, \*\*Walk-In Order, Physician Portal, EMR Orders, \*\*and **BCS Measure**.

        <Frame>
          ![2025 03 13 15 09 12 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-09-12.png)
        </Frame>
      - On the other hand while adding a new patient, click on **\+ New Patient** button.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Patient Information">
      - Fill in the patient details in the respective fields. Fields marked with an asterisk (\*) are mandatory.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 10 36 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-10-36.png)
      </Frame>
      - Select the **Gender**, **Preferred Language, Race,** \*\*Marital Status \*\*and **Ethnicity**.
      - Check the **Is Blocked** checkbox to block a slot for the appointment. Then, add the reason for blocking the slot.
      - Next, select the appropriate **Patient Flag**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Address Details">
      - Navigate to **Address** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 11 22 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-11-22.png)
      </Frame>
      - Enter the address details appropriately in the **Address 1** and **Address 2** fields.
      - Provide the **ZIP Code**, **City**, **State**, and **Country**.
      - Add the contact details in their respective fields: **Primary Phone**, **Secondary Phone**, **Email**, and **Alternate Email**.
      - Next, select the **Contact Preference** by checking the relevant options (**Text**, **Email**, **Fax**, **Phone Call**).
      - Finally, choose the **Location Preference** from the dropdown menu.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Emergency Contact Information">
      - Navigate to **Emergency Contact** tab.
      - Click **\+ Add Contact** button.
      - Provide a \*\*Full Name, Phone \*\*and **Relationship**.
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Guarantor Details">
      - Navigate to **Guarantor** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 12 15 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-12-15.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Guarantor**.
      - Enter the guarantor’s details:
      - **First Name, Last Name, Date of Birth, SSN (if applicable)**
      - Select **Relationship with  Patient** and **Gender.**
      - Choose **Same Address as Patient** if the guarantor's address matches the patient's. If not, enter the address manually.
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Insurance Details">
      - Navigate to **Insurance** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 13 17 Pn](/2025-03-25_09-36-34.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Insurance**. Fill in:
        - **Insurance Rank** (Primary, Secondary)
        - **Insurance Type** (Medical, Vision, Dental, etc.)
        - **Relationship to Policy Holder** (Self, Spouse, Child, etc.)
        - **Insurance Carrier** (Search or manually enter)
        - **Policy Number, Group Number**
      - Enter financial details:
        - **Remaining Deductible, Coinsurance, Copay, Out-of-Pocket**
      - Click **Save** to update the insurance record
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Employer Details">
      - Navigate to **Employer** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 13 43 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-13-17.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click **\+ Add Employer**.
      - Enter:
        - **Employer Name, Website, Phone Number.**
        - **Email, Alternative Email, Fax.**
        - **Address (Street Name, Zip Code, City, State).**
      - Click **Save** to confirm.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Notes ">
      - Navigate to **Notes** tab.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 13 43 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-13-43.png)
      </Frame>
      - Click the **Add Note** button.
      - Type a note in the text box provided.
      - Select a note type:
        - **Important** (Red), **Warning** (Yellow), **Information** (Blue).
      - Click **Add** to save the note.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
