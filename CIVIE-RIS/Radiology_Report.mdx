## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" />  **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** a radiology report, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist:

<CardGroup cols="3">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **MRN**, **Name**, **Date of Birth (DOB)** etc.
    - Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="filter">
    - Click on the **Filters** panel on the right and select from the following options: **Alerts, MRN, patient Name, or Referrng Doctor, Date of Birth (DOB),** etc.
    - Click **Apply** to update the results based on the selected filters.
    - To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Worklist Dropdown" icon="list">
    Refine the patient list using the **Worklist Range** dropdown and additional category filters.

    - Select the desired date range from the **Worklist Range** dropdown (e.g., **Today**, **Last Week**, **Custom Date Range**).
    - Use the **Worklist Category** dropdown to further narrow down the selection based on specific worklist criteria.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## **Action Button**

<Accordion title="Radiology Report Actions ‚Äì Icon Functions" icon="send">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="üì§ Send Report">
      Click the **Send Report** icon to initiate or re-initiate delivery of the radiology report.

      The system sends the report to the intended recipient using the selected method (e.g., **Fax**, **Email**, or **Portal**).

      <Frame>
        ![T1 Pn](/images/t1.png)
      </Frame>
      - This is commonly used when:
        - The report is being sent for the first time.
        - A previous delivery attempt failed and needs to be retried.
    </Step>
    <Step title="üîÅ Requeue">
      Click the **Requeue** icon to place the report back into the system's delivery queue.

      - This is helpful when a delivery attempt failed or timed out.
      - The system will attempt to resend the report automatically.
    </Step>
    <Step title="üåê Move to External Queue">
      Use the **Move to External Queue** icon to transfer the report to a queue meant for external recipients (e.g., third-party vendors, referring clinics).

      - This is used when internal routing is not applicable.
    </Step>
    <Step title="üö´ Move to Invalid Queue">
      Click the **Move to Invalid Queue** icon to mark the report as invalid or unprocessable.

      - This is typically used when recipient information is incorrect or missing.
      - Once moved, the report is excluded from standard processing.
    </Step>
    <Step title="‚úÖ Resolve">
      Use the **Resolve** icon to mark the report issue as addressed.

      - This is useful when:
        - A failed delivery has been manually handled.
        - The report has been sent through an alternate method.
        - No further action is required.

        Once resolved, the report will no longer be flagged for attention.
    </Step>
    <Step title="üëÅÔ∏è View Report">
      Click the **View Report** (eye) icon to open a detailed PDF preview panel.

## **Editing Referring Doctor**

<Accordion title="Steps to Edit Referring Doctor/Physician" icon="user-check">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Access the Edit Referring Physician Panel">
      - This window includes multiple tabs: **Physician Detail**, **Practices**, **Security**, and **Notes/Comments**.

        <Frame>
          ![T3 Pn](/images/t3.png)
        </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fill Physician Detail Tab">
      <Frame>
        ![T2 Pn](/images/t2.png)
      </Frame>
      - Enter or update physician's **First Name**, **Last Name**, and **NPI Number**.
      - Select the appropriate **Degree Type** from the dropdown.
      - Fill in **Address1**, **Address2**, **City**, **State**, and **Zip Code**.
      - Under **Special Options**, check applicable boxes (e.g., "Is Facility User", "Need Fax Confirmation").
      - Enter **Fax**, **Phone**, and **Email** details.
      - Choose **Preferred Contact Method** (Text, Email, Fax, or Phone Call).
      - Select options for **Primary/Secondary/Tertiary Marketing Staff** and **EMR System**.
      - Add any additional notes in the **Notes** section at the bottom.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Assign Practices in the Practices Tab">
      <Frame>
        ![T3 Pn](/images/t3.png)
      </Frame>
      - Under the **Search Facility** section, use filters to locate the desired practice(s).
      - Select one or multiple practices by checking the box next to each.
      - Click **Add Selected** to move them into the "Selected Facilities" section.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Configure Settings in the Security Tab">
      ![T4 Pn](/images/t4.png)

      - Send a **Registration Link** to the physician via the provided button.
      - Toggle **Is Verified**, **Enable 2 Factor Authentication**, and **Is Active** switches as needed.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Add Notes or Comments in Notes/Comments Tab">
      <Frame>
        ![T5 Pn](/images/t5.png)
      </Frame>
      - This tab allows adding internal notes or comments related to the referring physician.
      - Click **Refresh** to check for existing comments.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save the Information">
      - After entering all necessary details across the tabs, click **Save** to apply the changes.
      - Click **Cancel** to discard any modifications and exit the panel.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
