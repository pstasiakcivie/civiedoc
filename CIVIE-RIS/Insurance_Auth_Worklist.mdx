> **Summary - Displays the total count of orders received based on different time frames:**
>
> **All Documents** – Total number of received orders.
>
> **Today** – Orders received on the current day.
>
> **Tomorrow** – Orders to be received on the next day.
>
> **This Week** – Orders received during the current week.

![Ina Pn](/images/ina.png)

Beneath the summary, it shows all the personalized worklists created by the logged-in user. These worklists are easy to access and manage, allowing users to view, verify, and take necessary actions effortlessly. This ensures better control, accountability, and efficient handling of orders.

## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" /> **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** an order, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **Order Status, Order , or Patient**.
    - Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="filter">
    Apply specific filters using the filter panel on the right to refine the list further.

    - Upon selecting specific filters, click the **Apply** button to update the results.
    - To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## <Icon icon="address-card" iconType="duotone" color="#090909" size="27" /> **Adding a New Order**

<Accordion title="To add a new order" icon="file-arrow-down" iconType="duotone">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Click the + New Order button located at the top-right corner of the Orders Worklist">
      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 25 09 34 09 Pn](/images/2025-03-25_09-34-09.png)
      </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select the Order Type">
      Click on the **Order Type** dropdown and select the appropriate order, by default selected order type is online order. User can change the order to – **Patient Self-Schedule**, **Follow-Up Order**, **Phone Order**, **Walk-In Order, Physician Portal, EMR Orders**, and **BCS Measure**.

      <Frame>
        ![2025 03 13 15 09 12 Pn](/images/2025-03-13_15-09-12.png)
      </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Search or Add the Patient">
      **Search for an existing patient** using the search bar provided if it is an existing patient.

      If it is a new patient [**Add a New Patient**](https://collaborativeimaging.mintlify.app/CIVIE-RIS/Patients#adding-a-new-patient) by clicking the \+ icon provided.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Review the Patient Information">
      Upon selecting or adding a patient, the patient details are fetched.
      Review the Patient details and proceed to add order
    </Step>
    <Step title="Search or Add the Referring Physician">
      
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter Exam/Procedure">
      Click the **Exam/Procedure** field and use the search icon to select the appropriate procedure from the list.

      <Frame>
        <img
          src="/images/2025-03-28_09-43-58.png"
          alt="2025 03 28 09 43 58 Pn"
          className="mx-auto"
          style={{ width:"81%" }}
        />
      </Frame>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter ICD Code">
      In the **ICD Code** field, click the search icon and select the relevant diagnosis code.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select Billing Component Type">
      Choose the **Billing Component Type** from the dropdown — options may include **Global**, **Professional**, or **Technical**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Set Exam Priority">
      From the **Exam Priority** dropdown, select the urgency level (e.g., **ROUTINE**, **STAT**, etc.).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select Radiology Group">
      Pick a **Rad Group** from the dropdown to assign which radiology team or department should handle the exam.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enable Wait List (Optional)">
      Check the **Wait List** box if the patient should be placed on hold for the procedure until further availability.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Assign Reading Doctor">
      Use the **Reading Doctor** dropdown to assign the interpreting physician for the exam.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Save the Exam">
      Click **Save** to add the exam details, or click **Cancel** to discard the entry.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
