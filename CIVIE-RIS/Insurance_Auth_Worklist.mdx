> **Summary - Displays the total count of orders received based on different time frames:**
>
> - **All Documents** – Total number of received orders.
> - **Today** – Orders received on the current day.
> - **Tomorrow** – Orders to be received on the next day.
> - **This Week** – Orders received during the current week.

Beneath the summary, it shows all the personalized worklists created by the logged-in user. These worklists are easy to access and manage, allowing users to view, verify, and take necessary actions effortlessly. This ensures better control, accountability, and efficient handling of orders.

## <Icon icon="filter-list" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" /> **Search and Filters**

To **quickly locate** an order, use the **search and filter** options available in the worklist.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Search Fields" icon="magnifying-glass" iconType="duotone">
    Use the search fields at the top of each column to filter results based on specific criteria.

    - Utilize the search bar at the top to locate a patient by entering **Order Status, Order , or Patient**.
    - Enter the search query, and the results will automatically update in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Panel" icon="filter">
    Apply specific filters using the filter panel on the right to refine the list further.

    - Upon selecting specific filters, click the **Apply** button to update the results.
    - To clear all applied filters, click the **Reset Filters** button at the top.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## <Icon icon="list-ul" iconType="duotone" color="black" size="27" /> **Creating Custom Worklists**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Worklist Panel">
    Navigate to the **Worklists** page and open the panel from the right-side menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Worklist">
    Click the <Icon icon="plus-large" iconType="solid" size="15" /> icon to initiate a new worklist.

    <Frame>
      ![Sa Pn](/images/sa.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Worklist Details">
    Provide a **Worklist Name** and **Description**.

    <Frame>
      ![Worklist Pn](/images/worklist.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Access Preferences">
    Enable **Add to Dashboard, Quick Search, or Main Menu** as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Filter Conditions">
    Configure **fields, operators, and values** to refine data selection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Worklist">
    Click **Save** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>
