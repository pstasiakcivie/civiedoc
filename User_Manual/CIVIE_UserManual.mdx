# Introduction to CIVIE RIS

## Overview

CIVIE is a comprehensive healthcare management portal designed to streamline workflows for patients, physicians, front desk users, schedulers, administrators, and super admins. The platform enhances accessibility and efficiency in scheduling appointments, managing patient balances, and ensuring seamless financial transactions. With intuitive features and real-time data insights, CIVIE optimizes healthcare operations while delivering superior patient experience.

## Core Functionalities

* **Appointment Scheduling** ‚Äì Allows patients to book appointments effortlessly, while physicians and front desk staff can manage, modify, or update schedules in real time.

* **Billing & Payments** ‚Äì Patients can view their outstanding balances and make full or partial payments or settle dues through structured payment plans.

* **Comprehensive Patient Records** ‚Äì Provides quick access to essential patient details, insurance information, and medical records, enabling better decision-making.

* **Physician Dashboard** ‚Äì Physicians can view and manage scheduled appointments, ensuring better coordination of patient care.

## Authentication

### Sign In to CIVIE

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a Web Browser">
    Navigate to the CIVIE URL - ([CIVIE URL]).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Credentials">
    * **Username/Email**
    * **Password**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Click "Sign In" to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Sign Out of CIVIE

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Profile Menu">
    Click on the Profile Icon (Top-right corner of the screen).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Sign Out">
    Choose "Sign Out" from the menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Logout">
    You will be redirected to the login page for security purposes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ### Security Best Practices
  * **Always log out** after your session for security
  * **Use a strong password** and update it periodically
</Note>

### Password Recovery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Password Reset">
    Click "Forgot Password?" below the login fields.
    Link will be sent to your registered mail address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Email">
    Check your email inbox for a password reset link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reset Password">
    * Enter a **New Password**
    * Re-enter the Password for Confirmation
    * Click **Submit** to save your new password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Reset">
    Once updated, return to the CIVIE login page and sign in with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>
